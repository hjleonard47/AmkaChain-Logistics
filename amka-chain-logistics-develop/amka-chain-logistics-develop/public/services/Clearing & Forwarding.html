Clearing & Forwarding

Since our inception, we have been offering unparalleled Custom Clearing services. We have been steadily consolidating our Customs brokerage services in the general cargo industry space, by offering Best-in-class Custom Clearing Services from around the globe at Kigali international airport. It is our endeavor to enhance our customer’s’ delight by offering the right solution at the right time.   Our Services Include
  • Documentation
  • Carting / Receiving Goods
  • Examination of Shipments
  • Project Registration with Rwanda customs for project shipments
  • Clearance of import cargo at the port of entry
  • Project Reconciliation after completion of Project imports
  • Complete documentation support to our customers to ensure compliance with Rwanda Customs’ requirements
  • Advisory services to our customers for effective packing for economic handling and safety
  • Handling of stuffing & de-stuffing at Ports, ICDs and Customers’ warehouse
  • HS Classification
  • Computation of appropriate Customs Duty Rates
  • Preparation of bills of entry
  • Processing the customs clearance, assessment, customs examination and obtaining customs out of charge and prompt dispatch / delivery of consignments to consignee location
  • Preparation of shipping bill (on the basis of shippers letter of instruction)
  • Classification for scheme shipping bills
  • Receipt of goods
  • Measurement & weighment of cargo
  • Marking & Labeling
  • Ensuring presence during customs examination and ensuring flawless ‘Out of charge’
