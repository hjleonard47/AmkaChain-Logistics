Air Freight

Amka Chain Logistics offers customers the option to purchase space on a per kilo basis. Our experienced staff will advise the most cost effective method of moving your freight. Calculating Charges:

Space is bought on a volume or weight basis, depending on which is greater. Airlines charge per volumetric weight. The formula used is to multiply Length x Width x Depth in centimeters then divide by 6,000. Either the volumetric or actual weight will be charged, whichever is greater. Sidoman can handle all of your airfreight requirements for import and export movements.
