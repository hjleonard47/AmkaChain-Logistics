Who We Are

Hello and welcome to our website. Amka Chain Logistics is a company operating from Rwanda providing local and global solutions to our clients. Amka is Swahili name meaning Wake Up.

We are christian believers in Aviation industry for more than 25 years. We got the wide revelation from God since 2013 of creating this company to serve our nation and the world, animated with bible principles that made the world what it is today.

Amka Creative group, aims to inspire Africans for Innovation and exploration of God’s gifts, resources and opportunities that we have never tried and believe it is naturally for citizens of other continents.

African Christians have been criticized of laziness and lack of zeal of work. Equipped with ever rich bible principles and authentic faith, we are determined to offer faithful and quality services to our clients and participate in the development of our country and the world.

We are a powerful global network and of course our sky is not limited. Our finances are maintaining good percentage dedicated for mission of good news of hope and liberty for spiritual, physical and moral development.

At Amka Chain Logistics we strive for excellence and our greatest asset is the men and women who work tirelessly to see that we achieve our goals and objectives.

"Try our services and you will experience the difference and together business shall get blessed and developed."
